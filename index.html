About

Hi, I'm H Pallab Jyoti Dutta, an AI Scientist passionate about advancing the field of artificial intelligence through innovative research and practical applications. I'm currently working at Ziroh Labs, Bengaluru, where I focus on developing a platform for inference, fine-tuning, and distillation of large language models (LLMs) optimized for CPU execution without relying on GPUs.
I have completed my PhD from IIT Guwahati, Assam. My research has centered around localization and recognition of hand gestures using different deep learning methods and employing these gestures for operating interfaces for hand rehabilitation and assisting patients in communicating with medical staff. Throughout my academic and professional journey, I've had the privilege of publishing and presenting my work at various reputed journals and international conferences, like IEEE Transactions on Instrumentations and Measurements, IEEE Transactions on Artificial Intelligence, Expert Systems with Applications, International Conference on Computer Vision and Image Processing (CVIP), National Conference on Communications (NCC), etc.
